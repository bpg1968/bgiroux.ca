## Ingredients
- 2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and sliced into 1/4 inch sticks.
- olive oil
- garlic powder
- smoked paprika
- salt
- ground black pepper

## Directions
1. Preheat the air fryer to 380°F.
2. Place the sweet potatoes in a large mixing bowl, and toss with olive oil, garlic powder, paprika salt, and black pepper.
3. Cook 18 minutes, turning half way.
4. Serve immediately with your favourite dipping sauce.
